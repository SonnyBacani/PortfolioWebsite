Bike Sales Dashboard

The dashboard shows the Yearly Total Revenue, Revenue by Location, Sales by Generation (ex. Gen Z, Millenials, etc), Sales by Bike model and Sales by Location.

Car Sales Dashboard

The dashboard shows the Total Cars Sold by Car Company, Top Performing Dealers, Monthly Car Sales ad Average Car Price by Brand.

YoY and 2019 Child Abuse and Neglect Dashboard

The YoY dashboard shows the yearly trend of victims, fatalities, and states with highest cases and perpetrators related to child abuse and neglect. Data is from HealthData.gov

2019 dashboard visualizes the trends in child abuse and neglect for 2019 across age, state, and perpetrators by relationship. Data is from HealthData.gov
